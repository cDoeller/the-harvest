The Harvest

Hooray, we've finally found a way to grow broccoli in the desert! The broccoli are transported via balloons, but unfortunately the landing mechanism fails. Help us and collect points by bursting the balloons with arrows. The harder they are to hit and destroy, the bigger the reward. But be careful! If you hit a broccoli, it will rot. If it disappears from the screen, it is lost forever. Both will result in a penalty!
